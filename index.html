ABOUT ME

I am a self-taught front end developer. I enjoy the challenges experienced while coding and find it very satisfying to provide innovative solutions through applications.


I find it stimulating to create applications and websites. I am passionate about how I can use technology to create innovative solutions and intrigued by how technology can be used to improve processes. I am motivated to become a leading front end developer and have shown resilience over the past 2 years in the hopes of becoming a successful developer. I am a self-motivated and determined individual.
